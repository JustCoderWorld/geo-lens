What is llms.txt?

    
llms.txt is a proposed standard: a single Markdown file at the root of your domain that gives large language models a clean, curated overview of your site. Where robots.txt tells crawlers what they can't access, llms.txt tells AI models what's most important — your key pages, docs and resources — in a format they parse easily. It's an emerging, low-effort GEO signal worth adding in 2026.

    
Where do I put it?

    
Upload the file to your site root so it's reachable at https://yoursite.com/llms.txt — the same place as robots.txt.
